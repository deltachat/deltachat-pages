Minimal requirements:
Android 4.1 Jelly Bean
or iOS 11, iPhone 5s or iPad 5/Air/Mini
or Windows 7, macOS 10.11 El Capitan, Ubuntu 18.04, Fedora 29 or Debian 10
or compatible systems.
